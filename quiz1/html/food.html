Culinary in Surabaya

My recomendation about culinary in Surabaya

These photos are culinary spot in Surabaya

Sate Klopo Ondomohen

This unique Surabaya culinary tour is beef, chicken, intestine, shrimp or marrow satay that is grilled and served with satay seasoning as usual. Even though it looks the same, the tongue will feel a more tempting difference in taste. The slices of satay in this Surabaya culinary tour are bigger and have been seasoned with roasted coconut, this is what causes Sate Klopo Ondomohen to be more savory.

Bubur ayam spensix

Chicken porridge is one of the foods that is often used as a breakfast menu. This is also one of the culinary tours of Surabaya in the morning. One of the famous chicken porridge is Sensix Chicken Porridge. Here, you can eat chicken porridge with a delicious liver and gizzard topping. The location is on the Gubeng highway, and is open every day from 6 am. The price, which is quite pocket-friendly, can be a tasty and economical breakfast option.

Rawon Setan

Rawon is a Surabaya culinary tour that should not be missed when visiting the city of Heroes. Mbak Endang's Rawon setan which is located on Jalan Embong Malang, Surabaya is the original and first rawon setan depot in Indonesia. Called rawon setan because this shop is only open at night. Rawon meat here is quite large and also tender. The taste of the broth is so appetizing. Located in the middle of the city, this rawon devil restaurant is never empty of visitors.
