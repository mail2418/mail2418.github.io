Tourism spot in Surabaya

My recomendation about tourism in Surabaya

These photos are tourism spot in Surabaya

Surabaya Zoo

This zoo is located on Jalan Setail, Darmo area, Wonokromo. Once the most complete zoo in Southeast Asia with a collection of 211 species and 2,236 animals. This zoo has several functions for visitors, including the function of education, recreation, conservation, and supporting research activities of researchers.

House of Sampoerna

This Dutch colonial-style building is located on Jalan Taman Sampoerna Krembangan. Although it is hundreds of years old, it looks like a magnificent building. The front has four sturdy pillars that resemble the shape of a cigarette bar.

Tugu Pahlawan

This building stands to commemorate the events of November 10, 1945, when the Suroboyo people fought against the allied invaders. The location of this monument is not far from the East Java Governor's Office. You can visit the location of this monument every day and it will usually be crowded on Sundays because it coincides with Car Free Day.
